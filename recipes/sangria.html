Sangria! Sangria!

Description

The best sangria recipe I've been able to hone to perfection for summer parties! I like to use a fruity Spanish red wine, such as Garnacha.

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 mililiter) bottle dry red wine
  • 1⁄2 cup brandy
  • 1⁄2 cup triple sec
  • 1⁄3 cup frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 1⁄3 cup orange juice
  • 1⁄4 cup lemon juice
  • 1⁄4 cup white sugar (Optional)
  • 1 medium orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lime, sliced into rounds
  • 8 maraschino cherries
  • 2 cups carbonated water (Optional)

Directions

  1. Mix together red wine, brandy, triple sec, lemonade concentrate, orange juice, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl. Add orange, lemon, and lime slices and maraschino cherries.
  2. Serve immediately over ice, or refrigerate 8 hours to overnight for best flavor.
  3. For a fizzy sangria, add club soda or ginger ale just before serving.
